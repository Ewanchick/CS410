PAGE / UI STRUCTURE

MAIN BOX - centered horizontally, decent amount of top padding
	> HUD - anchored to bottom of MAIN BOX
		> ITEM BOXES - centered horizontally, spaced out
			> ITEM SPRITES - shrink to fit box

	> BACKGROUND IMAGE - stacked vertically on top of HUD, same width, no bottom padding
		> elements on top of background:
		   > item sprites - appear BEHIND monsters
		   > monster srites - appear BEHIND hands
		   > player hands - appear BEHIND text boxes
		   > text boxes - TOP element

IMPORTANT !
dyanimcally changing hands: 
	> click on item in HUD
	   > highlight item in HUD
	   > update hand sprite
	   > choose from menu what to do with item > follow menu to complete action
	   > after action is complete (battle) item remains in hand unless HUD is clicked on again (as part of toggle)
	> if ITEM = LANTERN:
	   > lantern remains in hand as long as it is toggled HELD
	   > does not update background image except in dark room and only if it is used
	   > but this should be fine because clicking on it for the first time means:
	    > pop-up menu prompts USE action
		> hand sprite updates to hold lantern
		> USE action completes -> background png updates
		> lantern remains in hand until toggled UNHELD
